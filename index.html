Essential Moisturizer Ingredients Guide

Discover the natural ingredients that make your skin glow

Aloe Vera

A natural moisturizer packed with vitamins and minerals that helps soothe and hydrate skin.

  • Deeply hydrating
  • Anti-inflammatory properties
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Promotes skin healing

Shea Butter

Rich in fatty acids and vitamins, shea butter provides intense moisture and skin protection.

  • Long-lasting hydration
  • Natural UV protection
  • Anti-aging properties
  • Improves skin elasticity

Glycerin

A powerful humectant that attracts and retains moisture in the skin.

  • Maintains skin hydration
  • Improves skin barrier
  • Non-comedogenic
  • Suitable for all skin types
